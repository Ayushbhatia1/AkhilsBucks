The Schedule

Six days. Two mountains. One golf course. Zero brain cells left by the end.

Day 1 — Wednesday, 12 August

✈ Arrival Day — "Herding Cats"

Everyone lands at different times because nobody listened to the group chat. Akhil will send 14 messages about the itinerary. Nobody will read them.

  • Airport arrivals — at least 3 people on the wrong flight
  • Accommodation check-in — immediate argument over rooms
  • Grocery run — 80% alcohol, 20% bread and butter
  • Welcome beers on the deck — Akhil gives a speech no one asked for
  • Dinner in town — someone is already too drunk by 7pm
Arrival
Day 2 — Thursday, 13 August

🎿 Snowboarding Day 1 — "The Humbling"

Akhil has snowboarded before, which means he now thinks he's a pro. He will talk about his "technique" on the bus. He will attempt a black run by lunch. He will be humbled by 1pm. The mountain does not care about your confidence, Akhil.

  • Early shuttle to The Remarkables — half the group still drunk
  • Full day on the slopes — Akhil coaching people who didn't ask
  • Beginners lesson for anyone brave enough to admit they need one
  • Après-ski beers — the actual reason we're here
  • Dinner in town — everyone walks like they're 85 years old
Snowboarding
Day 3 — Friday, 14 August

🎿 Snowboarding Day 2 — "The Revenge Tour"

Day 2. Everything hurts. Everyone is suddenly an expert because they survived yesterday. Akhil will try a jump. It will not go well. Someone will have footage.

  • Shuttle to Coronet Peak — collective groaning on the bus
  • Shred all day — "shred" used very loosely here
  • Night skiing from 4pm — Friday night under the lights
  • Post-mountain feed — eating like we've never seen food before
Snowboarding
Day 4 — Saturday, 15 August

⛳ Golf Day — "Gentlemen's Disgrace"

Gentleman's sport. Ungentlemanly behaviour guaranteed. Everyone suddenly has opinions about their swing. Akhil will blame the wind, the clubs, and the altitude. Never himself.

  • Morning tee time — someone will shank it into a lake on hole 1
  • 18 holes with skins game — side bets will get out of hand
  • Clubhouse lunch — Akhil's awards speech will be undeserved
  • Afternoon recovery — hot tubs, naps, questioning life choices
  • Big Night Out — the main event. Akhil does not get a say in what happens.
Golf Big Night
Day 5 — Sunday, 16 August

💤 Recovery & Send-Off — "The Reckoning"

Everyone will say "I'm taking it easy today." Nobody will take it easy today. Last full day means one last chance to do something profoundly stupid before returning to civilised society.

  • Sleep in — waking up in places you don't remember falling asleep
  • Fergburger brunch — non-negotiable, medicinal purposes
  • Bungy or jet boat for anyone still feeling brave (or delusional)
  • Final dinner — roast speeches. Akhil will pretend to enjoy them.
  • Last night out — no one will remember this. Historians will debate what happened.
Recovery Send-Off Night
Day 6 — Monday, 17 August

✈ Departure — "The Walk of Shame"

Silence. Regret. Sunglasses indoors. The group chat will be quiet for exactly 48 hours before someone posts a photo that should never have been taken.

  • Check-out — someone has lost their passport. Classic.
  • Last coffee — everyone staring into the void
  • Airport — a broken group of men pretending they'd do it all again (they would)
Departure
