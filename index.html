Rules of the Trip

Non-negotiable. Drafted without Akhil's input. Ratified unanimously by everyone except him.

  1. 01
    What happens in Queenstown stays in Queenstown.

    Except photos. Photos will be used as evidence at the wedding speech.

  2. 02
    Akhil pays for the first round. Every night.

    This is non-negotiable. It was decided without him. He was not consulted.

  3. 03
    Anyone who goes to bed before midnight on Night One is fined a round of drinks.

    You flew to New Zealand. Act like it.

  4. 04
    No talking about the wedding.

    Unless it's to remind Akhil what he's giving up. Then it's encouraged.

  5. 05
    The groom wears what he's told on the Big Night.

    Complaining only makes the outfit worse. Resistance is futile.

  6. 06
    No group chat messages before 9am.

    Violation results in being on breakfast duty. And breakfast better be good.

  7. 07
    If you claim you can snowboard, you will be filmed.

    Footage will be reviewed. Lies will be prosecuted in the court of the group chat.

  8. 08
    Last man standing each night gets to pick the first activity the next day.

    This has historically been a terrible idea. We're keeping it anyway.

  9. 09
    Fergburger is not optional.

    3am. Rain, hail, or hangover. You will stand in that line and you will enjoy it.

  10. 10
    Whatever Akhil says happened — the photos tell the real story.

    His version of events is inadmissible. The group chat is the official record.
