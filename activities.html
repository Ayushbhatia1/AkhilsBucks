The Activities

Snow, golf, and questionable decisions. Here's what we're getting into.

The Main Events

Two days of snowboarding, a round of golf, and a whole lot of overconfidence.

🎿

Snowboarding

The Remarkables & Coronet Peak

Two full days on Queenstown's best mountains. The Remarkables has wide-open bowls perfect for Akhil to loudly coach the group despite being an intermediate at best. Coronet Peak is closer to town for a quicker escape to the bar when his knees give out.

Akhil has snowboarded before, which in his mind makes him a pro. He will offer unsolicited tips. He will attempt something above his skill level. There will be footage. Après-ski is the real activity. The snowboarding is just the excuse.

  • Day 2 — The Remarkables
  • Day 3 — Coronet Peak (+ Night Ski!)
  • Night skiing (for the delusional)
  • Gear hire on-mountain
  • Beginner lessons (no shame)
  • Akhil wipeout counter: TBD
⛳

Golf

Queenstown Golf Club

18 holes with the Remarkables and Lake Wakatipu as your backdrop. One of the most scenic courses in New Zealand, completely wasted on a group of blokes who think a 7-iron is a type of appliance. Expect sliced drives, lost balls, and scorecards that are generously "estimated."

Club hire available for those who don't own clubs (everyone). Akhil will dress like he's on the PGA Tour. He will not play like it. Side bets will be aggressive and poorly thought out.

  • Day 4 — Morning tee time
  • 18 holes — par 72 (projected: 130+)
  • Skins game — reckless side bets
  • Club hire (we're not golfers)
  • Clubhouse beers after
  • Lost balls: over/under 25

Après-Ski & Nightlife

The real itinerary. Everything else is just filler between drinks.

🍺

Atlas Beer Cafe

Craft beers in a cozy stone building on the waterfront. The "one quiet beer" that has never once been one quiet beer. First stop, downhill from here.

🍸

The Bunker

Hidden speakeasy upstairs. Cocktails, low lighting, and the kind of place where Akhil will order something embarrassing and pretend he chose it on purpose.

🍻

Rhino's Ski Shack

The quintessential Queenstown chaos venue. Cheap drinks, sticky floors, and a dance floor where Akhil's moves will be witnessed and never forgotten. Mandatory.

🎧

Loco Cantina

Margaritas that taste like good decisions but are absolutely not. Nachos big enough to feed the group. Someone will try to order in Spanish. It will not go well.

🍴

Fergburger

The 3am pilgrimage. You will stand in this line. You will question your life. You will eat the best burger you've ever had. Akhil will cry. It's tradition.

♨

Onsen Hot Pools

Private hot tubs overlooking the canyon. Sounds romantic. Will be 8 blokes crammed in, arguing about nothing. Someone will fall asleep in the water. Lifeguard on standby.
